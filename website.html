Our Steak Dishes

Ribeye Steak

Known for its rich marbling and juicy flavor, the ribeye is a favorite among steak lovers. Best served medium rare for maximum tenderness.

Filet Mignon

This tenderloin cut is extremely lean and soft. Often considered the most luxurious steak, it's usually grilled or pan-seared to perfection.

T-Bone Steak

A combination of two cuts in one—strip steak on one side and tenderloin on the other—separated by a T-shaped bone. Ideal for big appetites!

New York Strip

Firm texture with a robust beef flavor, this steak is perfect for grilling. Often served with garlic butter or peppercorn sauce.

Sirloin Steak

A budget-friendly option that's still full of flavor. Great for quick grilling or stir-frying in steak salads and wraps.
